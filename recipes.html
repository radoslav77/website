Apple and caramel

Caramel Creameux
115 g double cream
130 g caster sugar
175 g Caster sugar
150 g pasteurized egg yolks
10 g gelatine
625 g whipped double cream
Apple jelly
15# Granny smith apples
200 g butter
225 g honey
500 g caster sugar
250 g double cream
20 g gelatine
Method: Peel and dice the apple to small dice (5mm cubes).Cook them with butter until soft.
Cinnamon popcorn
50 g popcorn cornels
10 g olive oil
100 g caster sugar
80 g butter
1 pinch Baking soda
1 pinch of table salt
1tsp of cinnamon powder (5g)
Method: Place the cornels in a deep pan with the oil. Cover it with tray or something large (so that the popcorn doesn’t go all over the place ) once is popped leave it on the side to cool down a bit . In a separate pan place the sugar and butter, make a caramel then add cinnamon ,salt and baking soda pour over the popcorn and mix well till all of the popcorn is covered with caramel . Place caramelized popcorn onto a tray lined with silpat and cook it in the oven at 180ºC for about 5 min . Once they are cold portion it into a vacbags and sill the bags .
Caramelized meringue
200 g caramelized sugar ( left to set and cool down , then blitz to a powder )
200 g caster sugar to 118ºC
200 g egg white
Method: Make a French meringue with :egg whites and the two type of sugar , the use a small star nozzle to piped and dry them .

Rhubarb and Custard

Sable base
560 g butter
200 g caster sugar
690 g flour t55
Method: Mix all together till form a dough. cut in the desired shape between two silicon mats, cook at 180ºC for about 10 min or until golden.
Custard Dome
300 g double cream
200 g whole milk
150 g pasteurised yolks
100 g caster sugar
1 # vanilla pod
Method:Make a basic Anglaise cook to 85 ºC , fill in the purple dome moulds and freeze
Pouched rhubarb
375 g caster sugar
475 g water
80 g Grenadine
10 leaves gelatine
1000 g fresh Rhubarb
Method:Boil all of this ingredient, then add the gelatine place the cleaned Rhubarb into insert tray (dip tray) pour over the Grenadine jelly/syrup, cover the tray with aluminium foil and cook in the oven 150ºC till a bit soft ( about 10 -15 min ) Once rhubarb is ready and coldish cut it into a pieces the size of the metal ring (raspberry jelly one ).place the rhubarb into the ring pour a bit of the jelly over and leave it to set in the fridge . When set cut the middle out with cutter number two from the tall set of cutters

Almonds,cake and Choclate

Almond tart200g butter, softened
360 g caster sugar
4 # eggs
200 g Flour T55
Method:Cream butter and sugar, then add slowly the eggs and on the end flour. Cook at 170º for about 35 min or until knife tip comes clean.When the cake has been fully cooked ,with help of fork make random holes into the cake ,then pour over the almond mix with all the liquid and return in the oven for about 10-15 min more or until golden and caramelized on top . Freeze it in order to be easily cut
Caramelized almonds
120 g Caster sugar

120 g milk
200 g Almonds slivered
Method:Boil sugar ,butter and milk . Once all is melted and nearly boiled add the Almonds and mix well .

Candied kumquats
500 g fresh kumquats (whole)
1 pinch of salt
300 g water
300 g sugar
300 g Glucose
Method: Bring to boil some water, salt and kumquats -then wash through running water. Place the 300 g water with 300 g caster sugar -boil with kumquats inside. Once boiled add the 300 g glucose and boil again till the fruit is soft. leave it to cool down
Almond nougatine
400 g Fondant
300 g glucose
400 g nibbed Almond
Method: Place fondant and glucose in a large pan ,bring it to boil and when start to caramelise add almonds .Pour over silpat or baking paper , once cold break it into small pieces and blitz it down in Robocoup. Place the fine powdered nougatine on silpat and reheated on 180ºC till melted .Braek into a small piece ready for service .
