A little bit about me.

Hello there, I have been a Pastry Chef for over twenty-three years. And really-really enjoyed it. I am very passionate and creative, a self-driven person in constant outlook to develop myself. The important things for me in a dish is a test, colour combination, presentation and seasonality, as you would imagine the best products are in their prime when in season and I like to compliment the flavours so that the result is a masterpiece worth a million. Working in several iconic 5 stars London hotels I have built extensive experience and expertise which I put in action day in and day out. From fine dining to events and corporate conference all of my creations are made with only one thing in mind - how to indulge my customers as there is no bigger satisfaction than seeing my customers happy and appreciative of the job I have done. Please have a browse thru my webpages to get a feel of your own.
